A 4 Week Redesign of the Minnesota Department of Natural Resources Website. The first two weeks of the project were in collaboration and the final two were individual, focusing on reorganizing information on the page with an appealing UI.

Mark Brunner and myself, Daniel Decker.

The Department of Natural Resources is a tresure trove of information, however, there is a lot of it. As a design team we felt that the amount of information effective accessibility of infromation that a user may need. So we tested this with research and a number of user interviews.
